About

I am a UX/UI designer passionate about creating seamless, user-centered digital experiences that balance aesthetic and functional appeal. I thrive at the intersection of design and user research, where thoughtful creativity meets practical problem-solving to deliver designs that are intuitive, accessible, and engaging.

Currently, I am a Senior UX/UI Designer at Coca-Cola, where I specialize in designing scalable design systems and refining user flows for complex platforms. I am driven by the challenge of transforming ideas and user data into visually impactful interfaces that meet both user needs and business goals.

Throughout my career, I have worked across different industries, including startups, e-commerce, and academic platforms, honing my skills in wireframing, prototyping, and user testing. My work combines meticulous attention to detail with a broader understanding of user psychology, ensuring that every project I contribute to enhances usability and fosters connection.

Outside of work, I stay energized and disciplined as a gym enthusiast, channeling my inspiration and creativity into my hobby as a tattoo artist. When I’m not sketching new designs or refining my technique, you’ll likely find me immersed in the latest RPG, leveling up my gaming skills, or exploring expansive open worlds. Whether I’m designing interfaces or diving into my hobbies, I am always striving for growth and precision in everything I do.
